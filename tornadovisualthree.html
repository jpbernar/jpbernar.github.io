Tornadoes Generally Increasing in the US

Tornadoes have seen a rise in frequence accoring to NOAA data

Tornadoes in the United States generally are trending upward in frequency accoring to NOAA. However, the reasoning behind it is still speculated. Tornadoes are already the most prevelant in the United States. The upward frequency could be related to factors such as climate change, changes in weathern patterns, and even more accurate data collection over time.
